Strategic Sales Performance Analytics

Data-driven insights to identify growth opportunities, optimize resource allocation, and maximize profitability across sales channels and regions.

View Dashboard

Problem Statement

Delayed Decision-Making

Manual report consolidation processes slow down critical business decisions, causing missed opportunities in fast-moving markets.

Ineffective Resource Allocation

Without clear visibility into performance drivers, resources are often allocated to underperforming products and regions.

Customer Churn & Returns

Inability to proactively address customer churn and return rate issues directly impacts profitability and customer satisfaction.

Solution & Objectives

Identify Growth Levers

Pinpoint top-performing products, regions, and customer segments to focus resources on high-potential areas.

Diagnose Operational Issues

Analyze return rates and customer behavior to identify and mitigate sources of revenue loss.

Track Performance Trends

Monitor Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and Average Order Value (AOV) to measure business health.

Methodology & Process

Data Acquisition & Cleaning

Raw sales data extracted from the SQL database and transformed for analysis.

Data Modeling

Star-schema with Fact_Sales and dimensions (Date, Product, Customer, Region).

DAX & Measure Engineering

Created KPIs like Total Sales LY, YoY Growth, Return Rate, and AOV.

SQL Example

WITH SalesByYear AS (
    SELECT
        ProductName,
        YEAR(OrderDate) AS OrderYear,
        SUM(SalesAmount) AS TotalSales
    FROM Fact_Sales fs
    JOIN Dim_Product dp ON fs.ProductKey = dp.ProductKey
    GROUP BY ProductName, YEAR(OrderDate)
)
SELECT
    ProductName,
    OrderYear,
    TotalSales,
    LAG(TotalSales) OVER (PARTITION BY ProductName ORDER BY OrderYear) AS SalesLY,
    (TotalSales - LAG(TotalSales) OVER (PARTITION BY ProductName ORDER BY OrderYear)) /
    LAG(TotalSales) OVER (PARTition BY ProductName ORDER BY OrderYear) * 100 AS YoYGrowth
FROM SalesByYear
ORDER BY ProductName, OrderYear;
